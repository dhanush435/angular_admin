Fun Tips:

  • Change your password regularly. Like your underwear. But don't use the same password for both!

  • Two-factor authentication is like having a bouncer at the VIP entrance of your online party. Keep the hackers waiting in line!

  • Using public Wi-Fi without protection is like playing hide and seek in a glass house. Everyone can see you, and it's not as fun as it sounds.

  • Ignoring software updates is like refusing to visit the doctor. Sure, you might feel fine now, but you'll regret it later when everything falls apart
